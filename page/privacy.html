Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 25, 2026 | Effective for all awjunaid.com services

1. Our Commitment to Privacy

At awjunaid.com ("we," "us," or "our"), your privacy is paramount. As a cybersecurity professional, I apply the same rigorous standards to data protection that I advocate in security research. This policy outlines how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you visit our website, subscribe to our content, or engage with our membership community.

Security-first approach: We employ industry-standard encryption (TLS 1.3), never sell personal data, and limit collection to what's necessary for delivering our security research and services.

2. Information We Collect

Personal Information

Name, email address, and payment details (when subscribing to membership). We use Stripe for payments — your financial data is never stored on our servers.

Usage Data

IP addresses, browser type, pages visited, and referral sources — collected anonymously via privacy-respecting analytics to improve our content.

Communication Data

Email correspondence, newsletter engagement, and membership forum activity — used solely to provide the services you've requested.

Technical Logs

Server logs are retained for 30 days for security monitoring and incident response — consistent with cybersecurity best practices.

3. How We Use Your Information

Your data enables us to deliver a personalized, secure experience across the awjunaid ecosystem:

  • Deliver Services: Process memberships, send research updates, and provide access to exclusive security toolkits.
  • Improve Content: Analyze engagement metrics to produce relevant cybersecurity and quantum research articles.
  • Security & Compliance: Detect and prevent fraudulent activity, enforce our terms, and maintain platform integrity.
  • Communication: Respond to inquiries, share critical security updates, and notify you about policy changes.

4. Data Sharing & Third Parties

We never sell your personal information. Limited data sharing occurs only with essential service providers:

  • Stripe: Payment processing for memberships (PCI-compliant).
  • Brevo (Sendinblue): Email newsletter delivery — you can unsubscribe at any time.
  • GitHub & Community Platforms: For members-only repositories and private Discord/Slack access.
  • Legal Compliance: When required by law or to protect our rights and users' safety.

All third-party processors adhere to strict confidentiality and security obligations.

5. Cookies & Tracking Technologies

We use minimal, essential cookies to enhance functionality and security:

  • Essential Cookies: Session management, theme preference (dark/light mode).
  • Analytics: Privacy-focused, anonymized metrics to understand content performance (no cross-site tracking).

You can manage cookie preferences through your browser settings. Disabling essential cookies may affect site functionality.

6. Data Security & Retention

Encryption: All data transmitted via HTTPS with TLS 1.3. Member areas require strong authentication.

Retention: Personal data retained as long as your account is active or as needed to provide services. Inactive accounts are anonymized after 24 months. You may request deletion at any time.

Incident Response: In the unlikely event of a breach, affected users will be notified within 72 hours per GDPR standards.

7. Your Rights & Choices

Depending on your location (GDPR, CCPA, etc.), you have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal data we hold.
  • Rectification: Correct inaccurate or incomplete information.
  • Erasure: Request deletion of your data (subject to legal obligations).
  • Opt-out: Unsubscribe from marketing emails via the link in any newsletter.
  • Data Portability: Receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format.

To exercise these rights, contact us at privacy@awjunaid.com or use the contact form on this site.

8. Children's Privacy

Our content is directed at cybersecurity professionals and researchers. We do not knowingly collect information from individuals under 16 years of age. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us immediately.

9. International Data Transfers

awjunaid.com operates globally. Your information may be processed in countries with different data protection laws. We implement Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs) and other safeguards to ensure adequate protection for international transfers.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. Material changes will be communicated via email or prominent notice on our website. The "Last updated" date at the top indicates when this policy was last revised.

11. Contact Information

For privacy-related inquiries, data requests, or concerns, please reach out:

  • Email: awjunaid@proton.me
  • Response Time: We aim to respond within 7 business days.
